        <dd>The SCP-8403 is a female human-like entity that is mostly see-through and can be changed by people who look at it and are close by. Its body can be altered physically, like changing its hair color or clothes, just by someone imagining the change. It's thought to be detached from normal reality, which is why people can influence it like a reality-bender, but only its own body. Over time, its see-through and un-touchable qualities are fading, making it more solid and less able to be changed.</dd>

        <dd>The Nika riots were an extremely violent week-long uprising against Byzantine Emperor Justinian I in Constantinople in 532 AD, considered the most destructive in the city's history, resulting in widespread burning and tens of thousands of deaths. The riots began when the "Blues" and "Greens," rival chariot racing fan groups who also served as outlets for social and political grievances, united under the chant "Nika" (meaning "Victory!") after two of their members, spared from execution, were under guard. This unity was fueled by public discontent over high taxes, alleged corruption by Justinian's officials, and his attempts to curb the factions' power. The rioters demanded the dismissal of key officials and eventually declared a new emperor, Hypatius. Empress Theodora famously persuaded Justinian not to flee, and the rebellion was brutally suppressed when imperial troops, led by generals Belisarius and Mundus, trapped and slaughtered the rioters in the Hippodrome, killing an estimated 30,000 people. Following the riots, Justinian executed Hypatius and confiscated the property of supporting senators, then rebuilt much of Constantinople, including the Hagia Sophia.</dd>
