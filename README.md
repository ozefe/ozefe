        <dd>SCP-2619 is a group of anomalous mockingbirds, with most instances appearing as various other bird species despite being genetically mockingbirds. One specific instance, SCP-2619-1, is a male Northern Mockingbird that can transform into any bird species preferred by a human within a 16-meter range, even extinct ones, with its DNA fully changing to match its current form. However, if a person dislikes birds, SCP-2619-1 will become their least favorite bird and act aggressively. All instances are kept in a standard aviary and fed insects.</dd>

        <dd>The AVE Mizar was a roadable aircraft, or "flying car," developed by Advanced Vehicle Engineers (AVE) between 1971 and 1973. It was created by combining the rear section of a Cessna Skymaster aircraft with a Ford Pinto car, intending to use both the car and aircraft engines for a shorter takeoff. The design allowed the car to be detached from the airframe for ground travel. However, the project ended tragically on September 11, 1973, when a prototype crashed during a test flight due to the right wing strut detaching, killing its inventor Henry Smolinski and AVE Vice President Harold Blake. Investigations revealed poor aircraft design, loose parts, and bad welds contributed to the failure.</dd>
