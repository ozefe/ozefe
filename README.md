        <dd>SCP-7041 refers to various temporary alternate versions of the city Chongqing, China, which appear and disappear through small openings in the regular world and gradually fall apart over a few weeks. These dimensions are sought after by the SCP Foundation because they contain SCP-7041-2, a small metal heart that gives off a special type of radiation called Frith radiation, which can stop or weaken dangerous space-time anomalies. However, a man known as SCP-7041-1 constantly carries SCP-7041-2 and uses his advanced agility, knowledge of the city, and ability to see a little into the future to evade all attempts at capture, believing the "heart" is misleading everyone and wants to be contained for its own mysterious reasons.</dd>

        <dd>A "rat king" is a rare phenomenon where a group of rats (or sometimes mice or squirrels) have their tails intertwined and bound together, often by sticky substances like sap or feces, or even by hair. Historically, the term was not always literal, sometimes referring to people who lived off others, but it later came to describe this physical entanglement. While some historical specimens might be hoaxes, modern sightings, including live examples, suggest it is a natural, albeit extremely rare, occurrence, typically happening in confined spaces during winter when rats huddle together.</dd>
