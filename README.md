This molasses-filled tunnel system slowly breaches into and converts surrounding underground spaces like sewers and basements, incorporating them into itself. The tunnels are also home to SCP-4924-A, a horrific, mobile creature made of dozens of human, horse, dog, and cat remains grafted together, which actively hunts living beings, lures them with distress calls, and attempts to pull them into the molasses to add them to its own mass or fuse them to the tunnel walls, where they remain alive and conscious, begging for death.</dd>

        <dd>The Cagots were a historically persecuted minority group in Western France and Northern Spain, first appearing around 1,000 CE. Their origins are uncertain, with theories ranging from descendants of lepers or heretics to a fallen guild of carpenters, but they had no distinct physical or cultural traits from the general population, with their only distinguishing feature being their lineage. They faced severe social segregation, restrictions on marriage and occupation (often limited to trades like carpentry or masonry), and discriminatory practices in religious settings, such as separate church entrances and holy water fonts. Despite laws and papal bulls aimed at protecting them, prejudice persisted for centuries, only significantly declining in the 19th and 20th centuries, leading to their assimilation into the general population, with very little of their unique culture remaining today.</dd>
