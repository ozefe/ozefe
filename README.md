        <dd>SCP-1363 is a highly contagious human virus that causes strange skin lesions. These lesions start as scrapes but eventually form words and sentences on the infected person's skin, which are controlled by two distinct personalities, SCP-1363-1-A and SCP-1363-1-B. These personalities claim to be two deceased nuclear physicists from the 1940s and can communicate through the lesions, even though the infected person feels panic during the rearrangement of words. While the infection can cause severe health issues and even death in some cases, most people recover fully within several months, with the virus completely disappearing from their bodies. A small sample of the virus is kept in refrigerated storage under strict biohazard protocols, and interviews with the entities are conducted while infected subjects are sedated.</dd>

These are also known as nested islands or lakes. The concept extends to multiple levels of recursion, such as an island in a lake that is on an island, or even an island in a lake on an island in a lake on an island.
