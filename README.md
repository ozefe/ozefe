        <dd>SCP-9173 is a granite statue in Galveston, Texas, that constantly projects a reality-bending effect, making things more "normal" within a certain radius. This effect can also sporadically extend far beyond its usual range, causing significant, sometimes catastrophic, changes to reality to enforce "normality," which can include reverting objects to older states, altering historical events, or even terraforming Mars back to its original barren state. The Foundation initially used it to prevent larger anomalous events, but after a particularly devastating incident in 1915 where it caused a hurricane to wipe out Galveston, and later predicted future events like the reversal of Mars' terraformation, it was reclassified as extremely dangerous, and efforts are now focused on destroying it.</dd>
