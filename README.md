        <dd>SCP-8357 is a small, heart-shaped terracotta figurine head, measuring about 36x35x20mm, with traces of pigment and what appear to be dry streaks from eyes, suggesting it once "cried." It also has markings indicating it lacked a headdress and has a fractured collar and earspool. At the time it was found, records matching its description mysteriously appeared in 73% of museums in the global north, though these records were later erased without any problems. The item is considered safe and is kept at Site-357.</dd>
