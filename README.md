        <dd>SCP-8376 is a seemingly ordinary steel shovel with a polycarbonate handle that compels anyone holding it to bury alive someone under their care or authority. Both the person digging and the victim, as well as any bystanders, become enthusiastically involved in the burial, though the victim's positive feelings fade once fully covered by soil. The shovel was found at a senior care center, and to date, no remains of the buried individuals have ever been recovered. It is classified as Safe and is contained in Site-56, with D-Class personnel regularly buried using it in the Site-4 Greenhouse.</dd>

        <dd>The Massachusetts School Laws, enacted in 1642, 1647, and 1648 in the Massachusetts Bay Colony, are considered the foundational steps towards mandatory, government-supported public education in the United States. The most notable, the "Old Deluder Satan Law" of 1647, mandated that towns with 50 families hire a teacher and towns with 100 families establish a grammar school to ensure literacy, primarily so people could read the Scriptures and resist what Puritans believed was Satan's influence through ignorance. These laws shifted educational oversight from the clergy to local officials and held parents accountable for their children's education, emphasizing reading, religious principles, and civic laws.</dd>
