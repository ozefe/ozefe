        <dd>SCP-7936 consists of 352 human corpses that mysteriously appeared in low Earth orbit on December 3, 2029, most of which were people reported to have died at sea, often by drowning. Each corpse is attached to a glowing octopus that shines brightly enough to be seen from Earth at night, appearing like a blue-green star. Attempts to bring these bodies down to Earth for containment have caused strange effects, including the corpses briefly coming to life and all lights going out in various facilities, leading to a halt in recovery efforts.</dd>

        <dd>The Mary Ellis grave is a notable burial site in New Brunswick, New Jersey, situated unusually behind an AMC Theatre on U.S. Route 1. It features a granite gravestone on a 7-foot high stonework pyramid, marking the resting place of Mary Ellis and seven of her relatives. Mary Ellis (1750–1828) was a South Carolina native, a property owner, and a strong feminist in New Brunswick, known for voting in city elections before women's suffrage was widespread. Despite a popular legend of her waiting for a sea captain, historians describe her as a pragmatic individual who requested to be buried on her land overlooking the Raritan River, a request honored by her niece, and the grave has since been preserved through various property developments.</dd>
