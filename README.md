This body mysteriously teleports to random coastal locations on Taiwan's main islands and cannot be destroyed. Its movements become more frequent around traditional Chinese festivals, and after each teleportation, its appearance, clothing, and surrounding items change, often including personal belongings or cultural artifacts. The Foundation keeps it in a special container with a tracker and covers up any sightings by witnesses.</dd>
