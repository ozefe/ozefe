While functionally impossible to contain, it causes severe degenerative skin conditions in about 1.2% of the population, leading to agitation, expiration, and sometimes unusual behaviors in survivors. This shrimp creates a sound reaching 210 decibels by snapping its claw shut, which is powerful enough to kill small fish nearby. It is found in the eastern Pacific and is related to *S. antillensis* from the western Atlantic.</dd>
